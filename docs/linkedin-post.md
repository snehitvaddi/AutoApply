I built a bot that applies to jobs while I sleep.

3 weeks ago I got tired of filling out the same 47 form fields for every single job application.

So I built something different.

I send a job link to my Telegram bot.
90 seconds later, I get a screenshot proving it applied.

Here's what makes it different from every other "AI job bot":

It reads my Gmail for verification codes.
When Greenhouse sends a security code, the bot reads the email, extracts the code, enters it, and keeps going. No other bot does this.

It works on 5 ATS platforms.
Greenhouse (271 companies). Ashby (102). Lever. SmartRecruiters. Workday. Not just one portal -- every major hiring system.

It picks the right resume per job.
Keyword matching selects the most relevant resume from my collection. AI Engineer role? Gets the ML-focused resume. Data role? Different one.

It handles everything most tools break on.
reCAPTCHA. Location autocomplete. Multi-page wizards. EEO forms. Country code dropdowns. Custom screening questions.

It runs locally. My data never leaves my machine.
Not a Chrome extension. Not a cloud service. A full autonomous agent running on my computer.

I battle-tested it for 3 weeks.
900+ real applications. 864 lines of documented learnings across every ATS platform.

The result: I went from 3-5 applications per day to 50 per day on autopilot.

I published the full setup guide -- all 50+ steps from scratch.

Slide through the carousel to see the architecture, every setup step, and what it handles.

Honest question: would you spend a weekend setting this up yourself, or would you rather someone just did it for you?
